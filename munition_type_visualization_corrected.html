Assessment Methodology

Important limitations: These assessments are based on remotely-viewed military footage without physical access to strike sites or munition remnants. Confidence levels vary by strike based on footage quality, explosion characteristics, and visible indicators.

Confidence categories:

  • Medium confidence: Multiple indicators present, adequate footage quality
  • Low confidence: Limited evidence, single indicator, or partial footage
  • Inconclusive: Impact moment cut from footage, extreme blurring, or insufficient quality

Note on nomenclature: AGM-114 Hellfire and AGM-179 JAGM are listed together as "AGM-114 Hellfire/AGM-179 JAGM" because these weapons produce indistinguishable visual effects in thermal video footage.

Analysis conducted by Joe Emmett, Explosive Weapons Expert, Airwars. November 21, 2025.
